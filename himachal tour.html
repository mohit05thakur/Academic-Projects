Tourism in Himachal Pradesh

Himachal Pradesh

Himachal Pradesh is a northern Indian state in the Himalayas. It is known for its scenic mountain towns and resorts such as Shimla, Manali, and Dharamshala. The state is famous for its beautiful landscapes, temples, trekking routes, and vibrant cultural heritage. Himachal Pradesh is a popular destination for nature lovers, adventure tourists, and spiritual seekers.

More Information about Himachal Pradesh

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Shimla and Dharamshala (Winter & Summer)
  • Known for: Hill Stations, Trekking, Temples
  • Best time to visit: March to June, September to December

About Himachal Pradesh

Himachal Pradesh offers lush green valleys, serene landscapes, and thrilling adventure opportunities. Trekking, skiing, paragliding, and river rafting attract adventure enthusiasts from across the world.
